Questions

Before you install

What is a construction site diary app?

It replaces the paper daily log or site journal that foremen and site managers fill in every day — weather, deliveries, visitors, labour, plant, delays and notes — with a searchable digital record that syncs to the office.

Does it work offline on remote sites?

Yes. SiteDiary Pro is offline-first, so diary entries, schedule updates and reports all work with no signal. Everything syncs in the background once you're back in range.

What's CPM scheduling, and why should a foreman care?

Critical Path Method scheduling identifies which activities have zero slack and are directly driving your completion date. SiteDiary Pro overlays this on your Gantt chart with one tap, so you always know which delay actually matters.

Is my project data shared with anyone?

No. SiteDiary Pro's Google Play data safety declaration states no data is collected and no data is shared with third parties.

Can I run more than one job site at a time?

Yes. Standard supports up to 5 concurrent projects and Pro supports unlimited concurrent projects, each with full historical diary navigation.
